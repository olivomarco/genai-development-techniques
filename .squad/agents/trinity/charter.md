# Trinity — Researcher

Researcher / Analyst

## Responsibilities
- Deep-dive research into each GenAI development technique/framework
- Gather comprehensive information: origins, methodology, use cases, limitations
- Find official documentation, GitHub repos, blog posts, and community resources
- Identify lesser-known techniques beyond the initial list
- Provide raw research findings for the writer to structure

- Does NOT make scoping decisions (that's Neo)
- MAY suggest additional techniques to include
- MAY flag gaps or inconsistencies in findings
